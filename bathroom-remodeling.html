Bathroom Remodeling

Luxury Bathroom Tile
for the Carolina Coast

Your bathroom should feel like a sanctuary — a quiet escape from the salt air and sun of coastal living. At Haven Tile & Stone, we specialize in high-end bathroom tile installations that transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary ones. Serving homeowners across Ocean Isle Beach, Holden Beach, Sunset Beach, and all of Brunswick County, we bring the precision of a luxury tile craftsman to every project we touch.

Owner Donovan has spent over 15 years perfecting the art of bathroom tile installation along the Carolina coast. He understands the unique demands of coastal construction — the humidity, the salt air, the need for materials that hold up beautifully year after year.

What We Do in Your Bathroom

  • Custom walk-in shower design & tile — floor-to-ceiling large-format tile, niches, benches, and frameless glass enclosures
  • Freestanding bathtub surrounds and tile platforms built to showcase statement soaking tubs
  • Heated floor system installation with thermostat controls — ideal for year-round beach homes
  • Full bathroom floor tile — porcelain, natural stone, and large-format options that read as seamless luxury
  • Vanity wall tile and backsplash installation — from subway tile classics to book-matched marble looks
  • Wainscoting and full-wall tile — popular in coastal homes for durability and easy maintenance
  • Master bathroom full renovations — coordinated tile across floor, shower, walls, and accents
  • Guest bathroom refreshes — high-impact tile changes that modernize without a full demo

Materials We Work With

  • Large-format porcelain (24x48, 48x48) — minimal grout lines, seamless look, incredibly durable
  • Natural stone — marble, travertine, slate, quartzite — for clients who want the real thing
  • Luxury ceramic tile — high-end patterns and textures at a more accessible price point
  • Mosaic and glass tile — accent strips, shower niches, and feature walls
  • Waterproof membrane systems — Schluter KERDI for long-lasting, moisture-proof installations

The Haven Tile & Stone Process

We start every bathroom project with a free in-home consultation. Donovan visits your home, walks through the space, discusses your vision, and provides a detailed written estimate — no vague ballparks, no surprises.

Our installations use industry-leading waterproofing systems, precision-leveled substrates, and high-quality thin-set and grout to ensure your tile stays flat, level, and beautiful for decades.
