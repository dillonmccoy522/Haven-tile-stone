Whole-Home Flooring

Tile Flooring Across
Every Room in Your Home

There's a reason luxury coastal homes favor tile flooring throughout — it handles sand, salt air, humidity, and heavy foot traffic better than any other material. And when it's installed right, with proper leveling, large-format tiles, and continuous layouts that flow from room to room, it transforms an entire home.

Haven Tile & Stone specializes in whole-home tile flooring installations — from new construction where we lay the foundation tile to total renovations where we tear out and replace flooring throughout. Donovan coordinates tile selection, layout planning, and installation across every room to ensure a cohesive, high-end result from front door to back porch.

Whole-Home Flooring Services

  • New construction tile flooring — full home coordination from slab to finish with builders and designers
  • Full home re-tile — tear-out and replacement of all existing flooring with new tile throughout
  • Open-concept living/dining/kitchen continuity — large-format tile that flows seamlessly across open plans
  • Bedroom tile — porcelain or stone tile in lieu of hardwood for low-maintenance coastal living
  • Hallway and transition tile — precision layout across doorways so tile aligns room to room
  • Laundry and utility room tile — durable, waterproof floor tile for high-wear utility spaces
  • Foyer and entryway feature tile — statement patterns and materials that set the tone for the home
  • Stair tile — porcelain treads and risers for multi-story homes with tiled landings

The Haven Tile & Stone Process

Whole-home tile is a major project that requires careful sequencing. We plan the layout across all spaces before ordering material, ensuring tile patterns align at thresholds, consistent grout lines carry through open areas, and color selections work cohesively across natural and artificial light.

Every whole-home flooring project starts with a free in-home walkthrough. We measure, discuss your vision, review material options, and deliver a detailed written estimate room by room.

Flooring Materials

Six Materials. One Standard.

We work with the full range of tile flooring materials — from entry-level luxury to top-tier stone — and help you find the right material for your home, your budget, and the coastal environment.

01

Large-Format Porcelain

24x24, 24x48, and 48x48 porcelain slabs deliver a seamless, high-end look with minimal grout lines. The gold standard for coastal luxury homes.

02

Natural Marble

Book-matched and vein-matched marble flooring for clients who want authentic stone. We source and install marble throughout the entire home.

03

Wood-Look Porcelain

The warmth of hardwood flooring with the durability of porcelain — no warping, no water damage, and perfect for coastal humidity.

04

Natural Travertine

Timeless and warm, travertine flooring adds character and Old World elegance to open-plan coastal homes — especially effective in large formats.

05

Luxury Ceramic

High-end ceramic tile in large formats and premium finishes — beautiful, durable, and accessible across a wide range of design aesthetics.

06

Quartzite & Slate

Natural quartzite and slate offer unique texture and color variation that no manufactured material can replicate — for clients who want truly one-of-a-kind floors.
