Tile Craftsmanship
Holden Beach Homeowners Trust

Holden Beach is one of Brunswick County's most private and coveted islands — a place where homeowners invest in quality and expect their contractors to match that standard. Haven Tile & Stone has been serving Holden Beach for over 15 years, building a reputation for precision, reliability, and exceptional finished work.

From full bathroom renovations in oceanfront homes to whole-home tile flooring in new construction, Donovan Haven brings the same craftsmanship to Holden Beach that has made Haven Tile & Stone the most trusted tile contractor on the Brunswick County coast. He understands the island environment — the humidity, the salt air, and the materials that perform beautifully in these conditions.

We service all of Holden Beach including oceanside, sound-side, and canal-front properties. Free in-home consultations available with no obligation.
