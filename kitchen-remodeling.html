Kitchen Tile

Backsplash & Kitchen Floor Tile
for the Carolina Coast

The kitchen is the most-used room in the house — and the backsplash and floor tile you choose set the tone for everything around it. At Haven Tile & Stone, we install kitchen tile that makes a statement: hand-crafted layouts, premium materials, and the kind of precision that shows in every grout line.

Whether you're updating a beach cottage kitchen or finishing a high-end new build in Brunswick County, Donovan brings the same level of craft and attention to every project. We work with homeowners from concept through completion, helping you select the right tile for your style, your light, and your lifestyle.

What We Install in Your Kitchen

  • Full backsplash installation — from simple subway tile to custom mosaics and book-matched slabs
  • Kitchen floor tile — large-format porcelain, natural stone, and patterned tile in herringbone, chevron, and more
  • Island surround and base tile — tile your kitchen island to match or complement surrounding materials
  • Countertop tile surrounds — tile borders, aprons, and transitions around stone or quartz tops
  • Open-concept kitchen/dining floor transitions — seamless layouts that flow between spaces
  • Breakfast nook and butler's pantry tile — cohesive looks throughout the whole kitchen zone
  • Range hood and vent surround tile — dramatic feature walls above the range
  • Outdoor kitchen tile — weather-resistant porcelain and stone for covered patios and pool kitchens

Tile Styles We Work With

  • Subway tile — classic brick, stacked, and offset layouts in ceramic, porcelain, and handmade glazed styles
  • Marble and natural stone — real marble, quartzite, and slate for clients who want the real thing
  • Large-format porcelain slabs — book-matched slabs that read like natural stone without the maintenance
  • Zellige and handmade tile — artisan Moroccan-style tiles for a high-design, textured look
  • Patterned cement and encaustic tile — bold geometric floors that anchor the space
  • Glass and metal mosaic — accent strips, vent surrounds, and niche details

The Haven Tile & Stone Process

Every kitchen tile project starts with a free in-home consultation. Donovan measures the space, reviews your design inspiration, and provides a detailed written estimate. We handle layout planning, material ordering guidance, and precise installation — from surface prep to final seal.

Our work is clean, coordinated, and done on schedule. We respect your home and your time — and leave the job site cleaner than we found it.
