Our Story

Why Haven Tile
& Stone Exists

Donovan started Haven Tile & Stone after years in the trades watching homeowners get burned by contractors who cut corners on the work that matters most — the substrate prep, the waterproofing, the leveling, the layout planning. The visible work looks fine; the invisible work fails within years.

He built Haven Tile & Stone to be different. Every installation starts with a foundation that would make another tile contractor nervous — because Donovan knows that what's underneath the tile determines whether it looks perfect for two years or twenty.

Today, Haven Tile & Stone is the contractor of choice for discerning homeowners across the Carolina coast — people who've been let down before and aren't willing to compromise again.

01

Craftsmanship Over Speed

We never rush. Every layout is planned, every substrate is prepared, every tile is set with the same deliberate care — regardless of project size.

02

Radical Transparency

You get a detailed written estimate before work begins. No surprises, no scope creep, no hidden costs. What we quote is what you pay.

03

Coastal Expertise

15+ years working in coastal NC environments means we know which materials and methods hold up to salt air, humidity, and wet-dry cycles — and which ones don't.

04

Personal Accountability

Donovan is on every job. You have his direct number. When a question comes up — before, during, or after the project — you talk to him, not a receptionist.
